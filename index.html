COVID Health Misinformation CTF

Graduate-level challenges in detecting and analyzing health misinformation

About This CTF

This Capture The Flag (CTF) exercise presents 25 scenarios designed to help graduate students develop critical thinking skills for identifying and analyzing COVID-19 and health misinformation. Each challenge requires you to apply statistical reasoning, source criticism, multimedia forensics, or algorithmic literacy.

⚠️ Content Warning

Some challenges contain potentially harmful health advice or misinformation. These are marked with HARMFUL_CONTENT warnings. Do not attempt any health advice found in these scenarios. Always consult verified medical sources like CDC or WHO.
